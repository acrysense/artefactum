about us

Artefactum Gallery — unique finds with a rich history

Artefactum Gallery — unique finds with a rich history. They say you can’t travel through time. Artefactum Gallery proves otherwise. Inspired by travel and archaeological excavations, world-class master in bench press, founder of the Punch Club promotion, businessman, collector Andrew Zaikin and powerlifter, WRPF’s President, world record holder, entrepreneur and blogger Kirill Sarychev created a unique project for the Arab Emirates called Artefactum Gallery — a space that unites the eras.

Artefactum Gallery is the place where you can find unique items with a rich history. Оur company is engaged in collecting rare specimens and creating real works of art from them in the form of luxury souvenirs, jewelry or decorative elements for further sale to sophisticated customers.

Artefactum Gallery is a unique place that unites epochs, past, present, and future meet right here, in the center of Dubai, only here you can find incredible exclusive exhibits that can become yours and replenish your personal collection by decorating the interior of your home or office.
