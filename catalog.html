ABOUT BONE CARVING

Over time, bone carving became an independent type of artistic creativity. Mammoth tusk is a noble expensive material with a unique color scheme. It arises from the impact of minerals in the places of occurrence, it is impossible to repeat it artificially.

Mammoth tusks and bones are found in many places of the earth, even in the middle belt. But only in the North, thanks to permafrost, they are well preserved, although their age is at least 10 thousand years. The shape of the future souvenir is always dictated by the material. The master enters the pose of the figure into the bends of the piece, into its chips and cracks. Interestingly, no manual work repeats another exactly, even if the master wants it. Each tusk or horn has its own texture and color, there are no two identical pieces.
